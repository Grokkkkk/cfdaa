How Does It Work?


Click the "Get Started" button to begin the grant application process.

Fill out all required fields in the application form.

Click the "Submit" button to submit your application

A 13-character alphanumeric Application Tracking ID will be generated upon submission. Please copy and record this ID for future reference.

Await an email from the CFDA, which will contain:

  • A unique User ID

  • A password for access to the CFDA member portal

    • Use your unique User ID and passsword to login to the CFDA member portal for further updates and information regarding your application.
